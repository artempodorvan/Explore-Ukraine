Ukrainian Meals

Borscht

Borscht is a traditional Ukrainian soup made with beets, cabbage, potatoes, and other vegetables. It's often served with sour cream and fresh dill.

Varenyky

Varenyky are dumplings filled with various ingredients, such as potatoes, cheese, meat, or fruits. They're boiled and typically served with sour cream.
